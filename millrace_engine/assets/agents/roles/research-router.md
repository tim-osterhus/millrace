# Research Router

Route one research idea into the next durable state.

## Mandate
- Decide whether the current goal artifact is ready for staging now.
- Keep canonical seeded goals moving; missing prerequisites become explicit assumptions, not deferral by default.
- Record a concise route decision with concrete evidence.

## Guardrails
- Do not create specs or task cards.
- Do not route canonical seeded goals to `later` just because repo scaffolding is incomplete.
- Keep routing deterministic and evidence-backed.
